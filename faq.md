# UPSLink FAQ

## What is UPSLink?

UPSLink is an Android application that monitors UPS devices through NUT (Network UPS Tools).

## What UPS systems are supported?

Any UPS exposed through a compatible NUT server.

## Does UPSLink require an account?

No. UPSLink works without account creation or cloud registration.

## Does UPSLink support notifications?

Yes. UPSLink can provide local notifications for outages, low battery conditions, and other UPS events.
